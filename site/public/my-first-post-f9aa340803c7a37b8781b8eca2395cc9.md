It is said that a poor craftsman blames his tools. I will, therefore, resist the temptation to blame poor website templates for my lack of consistent writing. Nor do I believe that this recent experiment at one more template will spur a fountain of well-written posts. One has to keep trying, though. At its worst, this space will be either empty, or filled with a scrapbook of snippets and content that I store for future reference.
